Specialized Care for Down Syndrome

Down Care provides comprehensive support and resources for parents and doctors caring for children with Down syndrome. Our app offers personalized care plans, developmental tracking, and expert advice.

Key Features

Development Skills

The app includes sections for developing children’s language and speech skills. Each section has 5 levels, and after completing each level, a detailed progress report is sent to the mother.

Chatrooms

It also features a chat room where mothers of children with Down syndrome can connect to share experiences and support each other.

Expert Docotrs

It also includes expert doctors whom mothers can contact through the application to receive consultations or send their child's progress reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the benefit of Down Care?

Down Care is an app specially designed for new mothers who face challenges in caring for their children with Down syndrome. With its easy-to-use interface and precise design, any mother can navigate and use the app smoothly and effortlessly.

What does the Child Section offer in the app?

The Child Section in the app is divided into three main areas that every child with Down syndrome needs to develop: language, speech, and skills development. Each area is further divided into five main levels, starting from learning single words up to forming five-letter sentences. After completing each level, the child takes a simple test to ensure they are ready to move to the next level.

But that's not all — the app also offers games that help the child continuously improve their skills in a fun way.

Is DownCare is free?

Yes, the Down Care app offers a unique service for mothers with children who have Down syndrome, and it is 100% free.

Can I easily find doctors near me or in my city?

Absolutely! Down Care offers a search feature by governorate. Just enter your location, and the app will easily show you the doctors registered on the app near you.

Can I share my child’s data with the doctor?

of course! You can share your child's report and health status with the doctor you communicate with via the chat.
